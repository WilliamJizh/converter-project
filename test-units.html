ğŸ“ Instructions

How to test:

  1. Make sure the extension is loaded in your browser
  2. Simply hover over any numerical value with its unit
  3. Or select the text containing the measurement
  4. A popup should appear showing conversions to other units
  5. Click on any conversion to copy it to your clipboard
  6. The new compact design shows top 3 conversions with an expand option

Note: The extension uses smart detection to identify units even when written in different formats (e.g., "km", "kilometers", "kilometre").
